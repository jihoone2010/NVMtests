New Views of Mathematics | About

New Views of Mathematics (NVM) is a math club dedicated to spreading mathematical knowledge, focusing on its usage and applications.

NVM members participate and plan various activities such as derivate bee, integration bee, math tutoring, and publishing math magazines.

New Views of Mathematics | Math

New Views of Mathematics (NVM) is a math club with special emphasis on the field of calculus. Although math is a broad topic, NVM focuses on calculus for its wide applicability. Calculus is intersected in many fields, including Physics, Biology, Neuroscience, Economics, and many more. NVM hopes to spread awareness of calculus in an engaging way so that more people realize that calculus is more than meets the eye.
